# Project Name: [Cross Grid]

## Project Description

A blockchain-based platform that enables communities to efficiently share and trade surplus renewable energy with transparency and security.

## Features

- **Community-Driven Energy Sharing**
- **Smart Contract Automation**
- **Built on CrossFI Blockchain**
- **Access to Clean Energy**

## Tech Stack

- **Blockchain:** [CrossFi]
- **Backend:** [Hardhat/Node.js]
- **Frontend:** [React/TailwindCSS]
